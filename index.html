About Me

Hi! I’m an aspiring Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in test automation, quality assurance, and client relations. Currently, I’m focused on creating dynamic, user-friendly web experiences with JavaScript, React, and other modern web technologies.

My journey began as a Test Automation Engineer at Sogeti (Capgemini Group), where I developed automation frameworks for KLM, collaborating across international teams. My work with tools like Selenium and Robot Framework has given me a detail-oriented, quality-driven approach, while my exposure to front-end tech inspired my shift toward development.

Before tech, I held roles in account management and system administration, building strong skills in client communication and project coordination. With a degree in Business and Languages and fluency in four languages (Dutch, English, German, and Spanish), I bring a global perspective and a passion for learning to every project.
My goal is to bridge my background in software quality with front-end development to build seamless, engaging web applications. Thank you for visiting my portfolio—I’m excited to connect!
